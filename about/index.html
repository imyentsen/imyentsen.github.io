I am a socially motivated designer addressing the challenging “wicked problems”.
My educational background has equipped me with the truly cross-disciplinary knowledge and skills. I am going to receive two bachelor’s degrees in design and sociology from two universities which are considered to be the best in their respective areas in Taiwan.
I work as a co-founders of dualai design studio, and on my own initiative, I participate in several meaningful projects including disabilities, learning, gender, agriculture issues. I enjoy the experience devoting myself to investigate, identify and solve the problems of our time.
My current goal is to design innovative playful interfaces for young children’s learning, which offer to eliminate the inequality of educational resources and enabling our next generation to act creatively in the transforming society.
